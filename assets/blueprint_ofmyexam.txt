*** RBSE CLASS 12 ENGLISH COMPULSORY BLUEPRINT (2026) ***

=== EXAM OVERVIEW ===
SUBJECT: English Compulsory (02)
CLASS: 12th
BOARD: RBSE (Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education)
MAX MARKS: 80
TIME: 3 Hours 15 Minutes

=== SECTION A: READING SKILLS (15 MARKS) ===
(Objective: Test comprehension and vocabulary)

1. UNSEEN PASSAGE 1 (9 Marks)
   - Length: 350-400 Words.
   - Question 1-6: Comprehension (Short Answer). [1 Mark each]
   - Question 7-9: Vocabulary (Synonym/Antonym/One-word). [1 Mark each]

2. UNSEEN PASSAGE 2 (6 Marks)
   - Length: 250-300 Words.
   - Question 10-13: Comprehension (Short Answer). [1 Mark each]
   - Question 14-15: Vocabulary (Find word). [1 Mark each]

=== SECTION B: WRITING SKILLS (15 MARKS) ===
(Objective: Test expression, format, and creativity)

3. SHORT COMPOSITION (4 Marks)
   - Word Limit: 50 Words.
   - Task: Draft a Notice / Advertisement / Invitation (Accept/Decline).
   - Choice: Internal Choice (One out of two).

4. REPORT / TRANSLATION (5 Marks)
   - Word Limit: 100 Words.
   - Task: Report on an event / Paragraph based on visual aid OR Translation of Hindi passage to English.
   - Choice: Internal Choice.

5. LETTER WRITING (6 Marks)
   - Word Limit: 100-120 Words.
   - Task: Job Application (with Bio-data) / Letter to Editor / Official Letter (Complaints/Enquiries).
   - Choice: Internal Choice.

=== SECTION C: GRAMMAR (8 MARKS) ===
(Objective: Test functional grammar usage)

6. CLAUSES (2 Marks)
   - Task: Join sentences using 'who, whom, whose, which, that, when, where'.
   - Format: 2 Questions (Fill in the blank or Join).

7. SYNTHESIS (2 Marks)
   - Task: Combine sentences into Simple, Complex, or Compound.
   - Format: 2 Questions.

8. PHRASAL VERBS (2 Marks)
   - Syllabus: Break, Bring, Carry, Come, Get, Put, Turn.
   - Format: 2 Fill in the blanks.

9. PREPOSITIONS / CONJUNCTIONS (2 Marks)
   - Task: Fill in the blanks with correct preposition or connector.
   - Format: 2 Questions.

=== SECTION D: TEXTBOOKS (42 MARKS) ===
(Objective: Test literature analysis and recall)

--- FLAMINGO (PROSE) --- (14 Marks Total)
10. REFERENCE TO CONTEXT (6 Marks)
    - Extract from a chapter.
    - 6 Questions (MCQ or One Word).
    - Choice: One out of two extracts.

11. LONG ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 80 Words.
    - Content: Theme, Character Sketch, or Plot.
    - Choice: One out of two.

12. SHORT ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 20-25 Words.
    - Format: 2 Questions of 2 Marks each.

--- FLAMINGO (POETRY) --- (14 Marks Total)
13. REFERENCE TO CONTEXT (6 Marks)
    - Extract from a poem.
    - 6 Questions (Explaining lines/Poetic devices).
    - Choice: One out of two extracts.

14. LONG ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 80 Words.
    - Content: Central Idea or Theme of the poem.
    - Choice: One out of two.

15. SHORT ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 20-25 Words.
    - Format: 2 Questions of 2 Marks each.

--- VISTAS (SUPPLEMENTARY) --- (14 Marks Total)
16. LONG ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 80 Words.
    - Content: Character sketch or Thematic question.
    - Choice: One out of two.

17. SHORT ANSWER TYPE (4 Marks)
    - Word Limit: 20-25 Words.
    - Format: 2 Questions of 2 Marks each.

18. MULTIPLE CHOICE QUESTIONS (6 Marks)
    - Format: 6 MCQs covering all Vistas chapters.
    - Objective: Check deep reading of the text.

=== TOTAL: 80 MARKS ===
